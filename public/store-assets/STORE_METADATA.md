## Full Description
*(Apple App Store & Google Play — 4000 chars max)*

Most productivity apps tell you what to do. Cinis is the one that knows why you haven't done it yet.

Cinis is an AI coaching companion built for people with ADHD, executive dysfunction, and anyone who's brilliant in their head but struggles to get things moving in real life. It's not a task manager that judges you for missing deadlines. It's a coach that shows up every morning, already knowing where you left off.

**A coach that actually knows you**

When you start with Cinis, it builds a baseline profile from your onboarding — your patterns, your blockers, the way your energy works. That profile shapes every interaction. Over time, your coach remembers what you struggled with last week, what you completed yesterday, and what keeps rolling over. You never have to re-explain yourself.

**Six coaching personas, one consistent voice**

Choose your coaching style. Whether you need a calm therapist-style approach, a no-nonsense accountability partner, a warm and encouraging mentor, or something else entirely — Cinis offers six distinct coaching personas. Mix and match up to three at once to build exactly the coaching voice that works for you.

**Proactive check-ins — it reaches out first**

Cinis doesn't wait for you to open the app. Morning check-ins land before the day gets away from you. Evening check-ins close the loop, roll unfinished tasks to tomorrow, and help you process the day. Your coach shows up on your schedule, not the other way around.

**Task management that understands context**

Add tasks by voice or text. Cinis parses them, schedules them, and tracks when things keep rolling over. It knows which tasks are external commitments, which ones you've been avoiding, and which one needs your attention most right now. The priority focus card puts your most important task front and center — no hunting, no deciding.

**Habits, journal, and finance — all in one place**

Build and break habits with streak tracking. Journal with your coach in a private, reflective chat that saves your history. Track bills, income, and upcoming payments so financial stress doesn't derail your focus. Everything feeds back into your coaching profile so Cinis always has the full picture.

**Your data stays yours**

We never sell your personal data. Ever. Your journal entries, tasks, and coaching conversations exist to serve you — not to train models, target ads, or get shared with anyone outside the services required to run the app (Anthropic, Supabase, Stripe, Resend).

**Free to start. Pro for the full experience.**

The free tier gives you the core coaching experience. Cinis Pro unlocks unlimited check-ins, deeper memory, and the full suite of coaching features for $9.99/month or $99/year. Cancel anytime.

If you've tried every productivity app and they all worked for a week before you stopped opening them — Cinis is built for exactly that experience. Your coach is ready. Let's start.
